JIT fulfillment flow
Mobile fulfillment SOP — follow this every order
① Order received
Customer pays via Stripe link on your vibe-coded site. Stripe email lands in your inbox. Screenshot the order details.
② Identify source
Check the product type. Made piece → studio stock. JIT Faire → place order on Faire app. JIT Amazon → order via Amazon. Digital → Systeme.io auto-delivers.
③ Place supply order
Order from supplier to your address. For Faire items: order only on day of sale — do not pre-stock. For Amazon: Prime next-day where available.
④ Receive, check & pack
Item arrives. Check quality — if faulty, contact supplier before opening packaging fully. Pack with tissue paper, brand card, and a handwritten thank-you note.
⑤ Ship to customer
Post via Royal Mail Tracked 48 (or 24 for premium orders). Send tracking number to customer via email/WhatsApp.
⑥ Log & update
Mark fulfilled. If Faire product: note the sale count here and recalculate days remaining. Check margin wasn't eroded by price drift before next sale.
